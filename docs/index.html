How do I transfer a file?

Open Vuiler+, tap "Send", select your file, and share the generated 6-digit code with the receiver. They enter the code in "Receive" to download.

Is there a file size limit?

No! Vuiler+ handles files of any size. Larger files may take longer to transfer depending on your internet connection.

Are my files secure?

Yes. Transfer codes expire automatically and files are only accessible to those who have the code.

Do both devices need to be on the same WiFi?

No, Vuiler+ works over the internet, so devices can be anywhere in the world.
